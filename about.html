About Us

Who We Are

Trusted Medical Store for Over 25 Years

Vyankatesh Pharmaceuticals has been serving the community with dedication, trust, and quality healthcare solutions for the last 25 years. Founded and led by Mr. Pravin Sarda, the medical store has earned a strong reputation for reliable medicines and patient-focused service.

Today, the legacy continues with the involvement of his son, Mr. Krishna Sarda, bringing modern practices while preserving the values of honesty, care, and commitment.

We ensure availability of genuine medicines, proper storage, and dependable service for every customer.
